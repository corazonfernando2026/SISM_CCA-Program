    Soka Melody Makers SISM Hiking Club

The Soka Melody Makers

Music with Meaning. Voices with Heart

To cultivate resilient, environmentally conscious students who appreciate nature, practice safe outdoor habits, and develop teamwork and self-discipline through regular hiking experiences. The Hiking Club aims to provide meaningful outdoor learning opportunities while prioritising student safety, well-being, and responsible adventure.


Learning Objectives:

By participating in the Hiking Club, students will:

  1. Develop an appreciation for nature and the outdoors
  2. Learn basic hiking knowledge, safety awareness, and risk management
  3. Build physical endurance and mental resilience
  4. Strengthen teamwork, leadership, and communication skills
  5. Practice responsibility, self-management, and respect for the environment
